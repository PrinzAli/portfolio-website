About me

My Story

I’m Aline, a Berlin-based UX Designer, and UX Researcher.

Combining my interests in psychology, design, communication, and technology with my love for details and the wish to create human-centered experiences in every situation, I am dedicated to creating solutions that will have users pausing to say: „that was one of the best experiences I ever had.“.

Diving deeper into usability testing methods like cardsorting, in-depth interviews, remote usability tests, and inclusive design, I am committed to learning new things every day!

I work in a focused, fast, and concentrated manner and am more than ready to make a difference in a dynamic team that considers the client’s satisfaction most essential. While working on various projects, consulting customers with different needs, and doing research for - and mainly with - a variety of users, creating the best possible experience for everyone is always my priority.
